About

This project was created as part of HackHI, the 2014 Humana Intern Hackathon.

Healthy Hi is a well-being tool that will provide you great insight on how to get your health moving in the right direction whether you would like to improve your physical or psychological health.

Healthy Hi is also able to customize a plan to get you to your best health by taking your hobbies and some personal information to set up a schedule of weekly activities. This will be just what you need to get to a healthier you.
